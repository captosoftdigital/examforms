## Cash Flow Management

### Critical Cash Flow Periods

**Months 1-6**: Negative cash flow
- Burn rate: ₹3-4L/month
- Minimal revenue
- **Action**: Ensure 6-month runway secured

**Months 7-12**: Approaching cash positive
- Revenue growing: ₹10L-70L/month
- Costs: ₹8-12L/month
- **Action**: Careful cost control

**Months 13+**: Strongly cash positive
- Revenue: ₹1Cr+/month
- Profitable operations
- **Action**: Reinvest in growth or distribute profits

### Funding Strategy

**Option 1: Bootstrapped**
- Founders invest ₹50L-1Cr
- Tight cost control first 12 months
- Scale with revenue

**Option 2: Angel/Seed Round**
- Raise ₹2-3 Crore at Month 0
- Faster team scaling
- 18-24 month runway

**Option 3: Revenue-Based Financing**
- Take loan against future AdSense revenue
- Pay back % of monthly revenue
- No equity dilution
